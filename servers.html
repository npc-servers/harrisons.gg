How to Join

Step 1: Install Garry's Mod

Purchase and install Garry's Mod from Steam.

Step 2: Joining our Servers

There are two ways to join:

  1. Click the "Connect to Server" button on any server card above.
  2. Or, open Garry's Mod, click 'Find Multiplayer Game', and wait for the Homigrad category to appear (not HOMIGRADCOM). Our servers have ZGRAD.GG in the title!

Step 3: Download Content

When you first join, you'll automatically download the server content. This may take a few minutes depending on your internet speed.

If you see errors and missing content, be sure to set "Allow all custom files from server" under the "Game" category in your Garry's Mod options.

Having trouble? Join our Discord for help!
